## Description (4000 chars max)

Palletium is the music streaming platform that pays artists fairly and rewards you for discovering new music.

**FOR LISTENERS**

• Earn rewards for every new artist you discover
• Premium members earn 1.5× rewards rate
• Progress through listener tiers: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond
• Complete goals and reach milestones on your music journey
• Build playlists and share your discoveries

**FOR ARTISTS**

• Verified artists earn $1.00 per first listen (200× the industry average)
• Earn $0.01 for every repeat listen
• Get verified for $49.99/year to unlock full rates and unlimited uploads
• Access detailed analytics on your audience
• Level up from Emerging to Icon status

**WHY PALLETIUM?**

Traditional streaming pays artists $0.003-0.005 per stream. That means artists need 250,000 streams just to earn $1,000. We believe artists deserve better.

Palletium's revolutionary payment model ensures artists can build sustainable careers while listeners are rewarded for supporting them.

**FEATURES**

• High-quality audio streaming
• Background playback
• Personalized recommendations
• Real-time earnings tracking
• Secure payments via Stripe
• Cross-device sync with web platform

**BETA ACCESS**

Palletium is currently in beta. Join now to be among the first to experience a fairer music economy. Your feedback shapes the future of music streaming.

Download now and start discovering music that matters.
